How to Participate:

Sign up by reading the MSHE MeetUp Rules of Engagement and completing the appropriate interest form below. Sign-ups will be honored on a first-come, first-served basis. We will do our best to match you with an alumni/student partner that meets the preferences you express when you sign up. While we cannot guarantee a perfect fit, we hope the opportunity to participate in multiple Meet-Ups will enable you to meet your learning objectives.

Once the matches are completed, you will be provided instructions for how to contact your MeetUp partner.

Lastly, at the end of the quarter, we will seek feedback regarding your MeetUp experience to help us continue to develop a MeetUp program that truly meets your needs. Please feel free to contact Audra Nelson with any questions. Thank you for your interest!
