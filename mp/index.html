Master's project aids, handouts, resources, rubrics, tools, templates and more.


The following guides encompass the major milestones in completion of the final Master's Project. They are presented in the order in which they will appear on your final project; however, some sections may be completed in the three quarter sequence out of order from what is presented below. Those highlighted in light purple are relevant for this quarter of the MP sequence. See the timeline for more details on when each milestone below will be due throughout the 3 course sequence.

Research Question

406, 407, 408

The guiding question that your Master's Project is aimed at answering.

Abstract

408

Very short summary of the Master's Project.

Introduction and Background

406, 407, 408

Overview and introduction to the MP including rationale.

Literature Review

406, 407, 408

Analysis of existing literature including gaps and shortcomings.

Data Collection Methods

406, 407

Data collection strategies and triangulation of data.

Data Results Summary

407, 408

Summary of data collected.

Data Interpretation

408

Analysis of data collected, especially as it relates to MPQ and Literature.

Conclusion

408

Summary of findings/master's project.

Poster Session

407

Presentation of project so far.

Final Presentations

408

Final presentation of master's project

Executive Summary

408

A concise, yet thorough, overview of your study.

Beyond the Master's Project

408

What to do when you are done with your project.
